    Academic Computing Services

    The Campus Information Managements System (CIMS) is the electronic respository of all data pertaining to the Division of Academic Affairs.
    The Coordinator of Academic Computing Services assists each College and each unit within the Office of Academic
    Support Services to efficiently collect and analyze data, generates the reports needed for university-wide decision-making
    and ensures an effective electronic registration process.
